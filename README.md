## Supply Chain Management Platforms

End-to-end platforms for managing supply chain operations and coordination.

- [SAP Integrated Business Planning](https://www.sap.com/products/scm/integrated-business-planning.html) — Supply chain planning and analytics platform.
- [Oracle Supply Chain Management](https://www.oracle.com/scm/) — Cloud-based SCM platform covering planning, logistics, and manufacturing.
- [Kinaxis RapidResponse](https://www.kinaxis.com/) — Real-time supply chain planning and orchestration platform.
- [Blue Yonder](https://blueyonder.com/) — Supply chain platform for planning, execution, and optimization.

## Logistics & Transportation

Tools and platforms for managing freight, transportation, and logistics operations.

- [Flexport](https://www.flexport.com/) — Digital freight forwarding and supply chain platform.
- [Project44](https://www.project44.com/) — Real-time transportation visibility platform.
- [FourKites](https://www.fourkites.com/) — Supply chain visibility and tracking solution.
- [Descartes Systems Group](https://www.descartes.com/) — Logistics and routing optimization platform.

## Procurement & Sourcing

Platforms for supplier management, procurement processes, and sourcing strategies.

- [Coupa](https://www.coupa.com/) — Business spend management and procurement platform.
- [Ariba (SAP)](https://www.sap.com/products/spend-management/ariba.html) — Procurement and supplier network platform.
- [Jaggaer](https://www.jaggaer.com/) — Procurement and sourcing platform for enterprises.

## Planning & Forecasting

Tools for demand planning, forecasting, and inventory planning.

- [Anaplan](https://www.anaplan.com/) — Connected planning platform for supply chain and finance.
- [Netstock](https://www.netstock.com/) — Demand planning and inventory optimization software.
- [ToolsGroup](https://www.toolsgroup.com/) — Demand forecasting and supply chain planning solutions.

## Visibility & Tracking

Technologies for tracking goods, shipments, and supply chain events in real time.

- [Tive](https://www.tive.com/) — Real-time shipment tracking with location and condition monitoring.
- [Roambee](https://www.roambee.com/) — IoT-based asset tracking and monitoring platform.
- [Shippeo](https://www.shippeo.com/) — Transportation visibility platform for supply chains.

## Analytics & Optimization

Platforms and tools for analyzing supply chain performance and optimizing operations.

- [Tableau](https://www.tableau.com/) — Data visualization platform for supply chain analytics.
- [Power BI](https://powerbi.microsoft.com/) — Business intelligence platform for reporting and insights.
- [Llamasoft (Coupa Supply Chain Design)](https://www.coupa.com/products/supply-chain-design) — Supply chain modeling and optimization platform.

## Sustainability & Risk Management

Tools and frameworks for managing supply chain risks, compliance, and sustainability.

- [Resilinc](https://www.resilinc.com/) — Supply chain risk management and resilience platform.
- [EcoVadis](https://ecovadis.com/) — Sustainability ratings and supplier assessment platform.
- [Sourcemap](https://www.sourcemap.com/) — Supply chain mapping and transparency platform.
